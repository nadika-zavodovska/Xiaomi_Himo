Xiaomi Himo C26

Electric bycicle

Complete freedom of movement and reliable transport for traveling any distance

HIMO C26 is a new electric bike from Xiaomi, which is designed for both city and off-road riding. It has a powerful engine and a capacious battery, thanks to which it is able to travel up to 100 km on a single charge, which is definitely higher than most competitors. And big wheels with pneumatic tires, disc brakes, a 7-speed transmission and an ergonomic seat make every ride that much more enjoyable.
